In this post, we show you how the Amazon SES Email Validation feature can help you to protect your sender reputation using two validation approaches: the Email Validation API for timely checks during registration and Auto Validation to automatically review all outbound email addresses before sending and only deliver messages to recipients that meet your selected validation threshold. Both methods are intended to catch problem addresses before they become bounces, helping to protect your sender reputation.
