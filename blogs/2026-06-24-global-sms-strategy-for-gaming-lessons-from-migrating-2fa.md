This post shares practical lessons from a real-world migration of a global gaming SMS platform from Amazon Simple Notification Service (Amazon SNS) to AWS End User Messaging. You’ll learn how to use resource sharing to avoid months of re-registration, how to build a country inventory that survives regulatory changes, and how to avoid the operational pitfalls that surface only when you migrate production traffic across accounts.
